Support FAQ

Common support questions answered instantly.

Call our Lost & Found hotline at +91 800-LUX-HELP immediately. Our operations team contacts your driver right away. Most lost items are recovered and returned within 24 hours. You can also report via the app under "My Rides > Report Issue".

Ride receipts are automatically sent to your registered email within 30 minutes of trip completion. You can also download GST-compliant invoices from the app under "Ride History". For corporate account invoices, email corporate@luxride.com.

Call our concierge immediately at +91 800-LUXRIDE. We'll locate your driver, assign an alternative, or arrange a full refund — whichever is faster. No-show incidents are treated as critical priority and resolved within 10 minutes.

Email corporate@luxride.com with your company name, employee count, and approximate monthly rides. Our B2B team will schedule a call within 24 hours to set up your account with custom billing, priority booking, and a dedicated account manager.
