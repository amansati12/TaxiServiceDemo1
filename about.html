Our Beginning

From One Car to India's Premier Luxury Fleet

Founded in 2016 by Vikram Malhotra, LuxRide began with a single Mercedes and a vision: to bring world-class chauffeur service to India. What started in Mumbai's business district has now expanded to 12 cities, with 120+ premium vehicles and over 50,000 satisfied clients.

We believe every journey — whether to the airport, a boardroom, or a wedding — deserves to be experienced in absolute comfort, safety, and style. That conviction drives every decision we make.

The LuxRide Timeline

2016
The Beginning
LuxRide founded in Mumbai with 1 Mercedes S-Class. First 50 clients served in the first month.

Fleet Expansion
Grew to 15 vehicles. Launched corporate accounts and introduced BMW 7 Series to the fleet.

Multi-City Launch
Expanded to Delhi and Bangalore. Launched the LuxRide mobile app with real-time tracking.

Electric Future
